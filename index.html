Questions Answered

Everything you need
to know before you start.

How does the gift selection work?
14 days before each occasion our team curates a thoughtful gift based on the recipient information you provided - their interests, age, relationship, and budget. We send you a preview email showing the selected gift. You can approve it with one click, or request a swap if you would like something different. If we don't hear from you within 48 hours the gift is automatically approved and ordered.
When am I charged for the gifts?
Your annual or monthly subscription fee is charged at signup. Gift costs are charged separately to your card on file when each gift is approved and ordered - approximately 10 to 12 days before the occasion. You always see exactly what you are being charged before it happens.
What occasions are covered?
You choose 3 occasions per person from our list: Birthday, Christmas or Holiday, Valentine's Day, Anniversary, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Work Anniversary, Graduation, or Just Because. You pick what matters most for each person in your life.
Can I add more people to my plan?
Yes - you can add as many people as you want at $39 per person per year. Each additional person gets their own 3 occasions, gift budget selection, and curated gifts. The Premium plan already includes up to 3 people.
What if I want to cancel?
You can cancel anytime through your subscriber portal - no phone calls, no waiting, no questions asked. Your plan stays active until the end of your current billing period.
What kinds of gifts do you send?
We source from a curated catalog of premium brands across categories including food and drink, wellness, home and lifestyle, experiences, tech accessories, fashion, and more. Every gift is selected to feel thoughtful and personal - never generic. We match the gift to what you have told us about your recipient.
What gift budgets are available?
We offer three gift budget tiers: Essential at $35 per gift, Classic at $50 per gift, and Premium at $75 per gift. Your subscription plan determines your gift budget. The cost of each gift is charged to your card on file when the gift is ordered.
